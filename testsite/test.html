About Us


The Florida Institute of Technology - IEEE Student Branch is an organization in which its members are given oppurtunities to learn and grow into both engineers and leaders. Our organization is made up of two different parts, both aiming to introduce young engineers to the world of engineering after college. The first half of our organization is devoted to young proffesionals and giving them the tools and information they need to not only get interviews, but to do well in them too. Secondly, we help students by giving them the technical skills that most companies expect graduates to have today. Teaching these skills provides students not only with valuable information, but also with practice working with a team of people.

About IEEE


The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. IEEE serves technical professionals and students who are looking to both foster working relationships and gain access to the latest technical research and knowledge.
Being a part of this huge organization gives our members a huge advantage in their careers. We are able to bring in the best guest speakers and provide valuable workshops only available through IEEE's vast network.
